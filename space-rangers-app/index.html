Welcome to the Space Rangers!

Welcome aboard, Captain! You just became part of the newest addition to Earths Space Fleet: The Space Ranger Corp. Our mission is to protect Earth and all of our colonies spread out amongst the stars.

Our primary duties will be fighting and destroying rogue fleets of Pirates. They're known to operate mostly in the Outer Edge, where the most defenseless colony planets tend to be. But they have been known to make their way far into Earth space. And the closer they get to Earth, the bigger the raiding parties tend to be.

As our newest captain, your first task will be to pick your assignment. After that, we will be granting you the exclusive privilege of naming our newest Battle Cruiser. You will, of course, be piloting this ship for the duration of the assignment.

Godspeed, Captain. The safety of humanity is entrusted to you.
